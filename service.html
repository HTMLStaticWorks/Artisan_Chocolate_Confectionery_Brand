Artisan Services

From curated tasting experiences to luxury corporate gifting.

Section One

A bespoke menu of curated chocolate services.

01. Private Tasting Ritual

For private suppers and chef collaborations.

We guide guests through a curated journey of single-origin chocolates, matching flavor profiles with botanical infusions and fine wines.

02. Curated Boutique Supply

For luxury hotels, boutique spaces, and dessert counters.

Tailored supply programs for luxury hotels, high-end boutiques, and specialty dessert counters looking to elevate their offerings.

03. The Tempering Academy

For teams learning live cacao tempering.

Hands-on sensory workshops focusing on cacao genetics, conching, and the art of tempering on marble.

04. Editorial Launch Bespoke

For brands that need an elegant, sensory focal point.

Custom chocolate installations, interactive tasting stations, and custom-sculpted chocolate centerpieces for brand reveal events.

Section Two

How a Veloura service day unfolds.

Each step is timed to maximize sensory engagement: from the initial cacao aroma to the final, lingering finish on the palate.

1

Calibrate the room

We map guest flow, lighting temperature, and room climate to ensure optimal chocolate preservation and tasting focus.

2

Curate the flavor progression

The tasting sequence is designed backwards from intense dark single-origins to delicate, custom-infused truffles.

3

Stage the reveal

Our team sets the natural slate serving boards, custom flavor wheel cards, and custom pairings to present each piece with absolute elegance.

4

Leave a sensory guide

When needed, we leave detailed tasting notes, origin sourcing maps, and storage guides so the single-origin appreciation journey can continue.

Section Three

Choose a format that fits the room.

Each service format is designed with a specific scale and pace in mind, bringing the rich heritage and fine art of cacao to life.

Salon Table

8 to 18 guests

Intimate chocolate pairings featuring five single-origin vintages and custom-matched botanical syrups.

Bespoke Boutique

Daily or weekly

A rotating selection of seasonal truffles and bean-to-bar collections curated for premium dessert counters.

Workshop Block

2 to 3 hours

An immersive masterclass for private groups covering tempering techniques, molding, and chocolate history.

Launch Night

40 to 120 guests

An interactive tasting bar featuring live truffle dusting, custom branding, and sensory storytelling.

We shape our chocolate services around your guest count, event timeline, theme, and pairing preferences.
