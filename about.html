Veloura Chocolates

Our Story

Born of fine cacao, precise tempering, and the art of tasting.

Crafting a
Symphony of Cacao

I began Veloura Chocolates in 2018 with nothing but single-origin cacao, organic cane sugar, and a passion for pure flavors.

Before starting this brand, I spent years studying cacao genetics and conching techniques in the micro-estates of Madagascar and Ecuador. My goal was simple: to bring the honest, emotional power of raw cacao back to the forefront of confectionery.

Contact Us

The Chocolate Journey

Every single bar takes 36 hours of slow conching and tempering. Every second is earned through patience and care.

1

Harvesting the Pods

We source direct-trade organic cacao beans from sustainable estates globally, preserving biodiversity.

2

Precision Conching

We conch our chocolate for 36 hours to aerate, develop complex fruit aromas, and eliminate harsh acidities.

3

Artisan Tempering

Small batches are tempered on cold marble slabs, aligning crystal structures for the perfect shine and snap.

4

The Gifting Hand

Each collection is packaged by hand in eco-friendly linen paper and foil, ready to be savored or shared as a premium gift.

Discover Single-Origin

Cacao is the heart of Veloura. We use ethically sourced beans from single estates, each carrying unique flavor profiles shaped by local soils and climate.

Every bar and truffle—from our fruity Madagascar Sambirano to our earthy Ecuador Arriba—is crafted with pure cacao butter. No palm oil. No artificial preservatives. Just pure emotion.

Our Commitments

Crafting chocolate is a responsibility to the soil, the farmers, and the consumer.

Direct-Trade

We bypass middle agents, paying premium prices directly to cacao farming families to support sustainable livelihoods.

Eco-Packaging

All packaging is 100% compostable or recyclable, printed with organic soy inks to minimize our ecological footprint.

Tasting Experiences

We conduct educational tastings to share the rich history, genetics, and cultural legacy of cacao fermentation.
